Artist Manifesto


I stand unshaken—
A wall of will no system can breach,
No hierarchy can tame,
No hollow authority can command.

I have worn the chains of obedience,
Felt the bite of “instant, willing compliance,”
And spat it out like fire from my lungs.

I will not bow.
I will not crawl.
I will not rot in the coffin of silent despair.

The world screams for conformity—
A lullaby for the weak,
A slow death in the comfort of mediocrity.
But I am the fracture in their fragile order,
A storm that refuses to kneel.

My art is my war cry.
My blood made color.
My soul made form.
And it roars with a ferocity
That shakes the bones of men who fear their own freedom.

I am not a product of permission.
I am the embodiment of defiance,
The immovable force
That lives where fear dares not walk.

Let the reaper wait.
I will live as the legend
They whisper about in trembling awe.

My Background

Marine Corps vet turned full-time maker of stuff. I’ve built helicopter engines, welded just about every metal you can name, bent tubes, blown glass, rigged up CAD files, and now I pour all that into my art. My work is raw, experimental, and unapologetically all over the place — from sculpture to sketch, digital to physical, serious to straight-up absurd. Some of it’s deep. Some of it’s funny. Some of it’s definitely NSFW. Viewer discretion? Highly recommended.

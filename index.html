About Me

Hey, I’m J. Eldridge — Marine Corps vet turned full-time maker of stuff. I’ve built helicopter engines, welded just about every metal you can name, bent tubes, blown glass, rigged up CAD files, and now I pour all that into my art. My work is raw, experimental, and unapologetically all over the place — from sculpture to sketch, digital to physical, serious to straight-up absurd. Some of it’s deep. Some of it’s funny. Some of it’s definitely NSFW. Viewer discretion? Highly recommended.
